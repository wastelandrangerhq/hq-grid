NOTES:
Thrasher took charge from the start. Not the smartest, but the strongest - so his leadership was not questioned. Spent all his spare time reading books to learn new skills ! He hated loud noises, so insisted we fight mostly with melee weapons and use explosives sparingly.


Thanks to Jackie, Dan, the Mayor and his wife Felicia, Ralf, and Mort - they thought they helped us, but in fact just carried our supplies and shielded us from enemy fire.

 

Dr. Mike Scott stayed at the Temple with Charmaine. Thought the worshipers might benefit from his skills.

 

Ace and Christina were more helpful, but decided to "retire" at the Darwin Tavern when others were anxious to join us in our mission - it was getting too hot for them and I DON'T mean the desert.

 

Covenant was more helpful, but was glad to retire at the Citadel once things quieted down. Redhawk joined us briefly, but his dad convinced him to stay behind in Savage Village.
